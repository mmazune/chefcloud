### Improvement Opportunities (Post-M9.3)

1. **Multiple Reminders** – Allow configuring multiple reminder times per policy
2. **Webhook Notifications** – Real-time push instead of polling
3. **Advanced Priority** – VIP customer priority in waitlist promotion

---

## Conclusion

M9.3 achieves feature parity with leading reservation systems and introduces unique features like **grace-period-aware no-show handling** that are not present in any analyzed reference system.
