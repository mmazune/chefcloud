## 8. Usage Instructions

When implementing any feature:

1. **Identify domain(s)** the feature touches
2. **Read the domain standard(s)** to understand invariants and acceptance criteria
3. **Check DOMAIN_TRACEABILITY_MATRIX** for reference repos and demo datasets
4. **Implement with E2E tests** meeting the minimum counts in Section J
5. **Verify all acceptance criteria** in Section I before marking complete
